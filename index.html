Till-Less Shopping

A revolutionary breakthrough in the shopping industry that is currently being developed by Amazon.

What is Till-Less Shopping?

Amazon Fresh stores equipped with till-less technology provide a streamlined shopping experience centered on automation and convenience. Here's a detailed
breakdown: Technology: The core of the till-less system relies on computer vision, sensor fusion, and deep learning algorithms. These technologies work together
to track shoppers and products accurately throughout the store. Entry: Upon entering the store, customers scan a unique QR code using the Amazon app. This
action links the shopper's physical presence with their Amazon account. Tracking: As customers move through the store, overhead cameras and weight sensors
in the shelves monitor their actions. The system identifies which products are picked up or put back. Automated Billing: When a shopper leaves the store, the
system automatically tallies the items they have taken. The total is then charged to the customer's Amazon account. Receipt: Shortly after leaving, customers
receive a digital receipt in the app, detailing their purchases.

How does this work?

Amazon Fresh stores use a "Just Walk Out" system to make
shopping easier.

When you enter, scan a QR code from the Amazon app,
which connects your Amazon account to your shopping trip.

As you shop, cameras and sensors track what you take or put
back on the shelves.

When you leave, you don't need to check out. Your Amazon
account is automatically charged for what you took, and
you'll get a receipt in your account.

The store uses cameras, sensors, and smart technology to
know exactly what you take. This way, you can skip the
checkout line and just walk out.

How is this going to benefit the
future of shopping?

"Just Walk Out" technology can make shopping better in the
future because it:

Saves you time since you don't have to wait in checkout lines.

Makes shopping faster and more efficient because there are no
cashiers needed.

Reduces waste by getting rid of paper receipts.

Helps stores understand what customers like, improving store
layout and product choices.

Makes shopping more enjoyable, so you're more likely to come
back.

"Just Walk Out" technology makes shopping quicker, easier,
and more convenient for everyone.

Will this save us money?

Yes! These are the ways that money will be saved.

For shoppers:

  • You save time by skipping checkout lines, and time is valuable.

  • You might buy less on impulse since you're not waiting in line.

For stores:

  • They need fewer employees because the system automates checkout.

  • They can sell more since checkout is faster.

  • They can better manage what's on the shelves, reducing waste.

The technology cuts costs by making the shopping process more efficient for
both you and the store.
